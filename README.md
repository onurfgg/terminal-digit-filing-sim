The **Smart Archive 5S Navigator** is a gamified training simulator and Decision Support System (DSS) designed for hospital archive personnel. It interactively teaches the **Terminal Digit Color-Coding** system, a globally recognized Lean Management and 5S technique used to eliminate misfiles and optimize document retrieval times in massive medical record facilities.

🔗 **[Explore Live Training Simulation (Live Demo)](https://onurfgg.github.io/terminal-digit-filing-sim)**

## 📋 Project Background

In large-scale healthcare facilities, a single misfiled patient folder can cause critical delays in medical treatments. Inspired by real-world archive management and data organization operations at university hospitals, this simulator digitizes the visual management standard. 

By assigning a specific color to digits 0-9 and aligning them based on the terminal digits of a patient's ID, medical staff can visually detect a misfiled folder from across the room—a broken color band instantly flags an error. This project bridges the gap between theoretical Lean Production principles and practical employee onboarding.

## 🌟 Highlighted Features

* **Gamified Onboarding:** Transforms the tedious process of learning the archive color standard (0-Red, 1-Gray, 2-Blue, etc.) into an interactive, engaging challenge.
* **Dynamic Digital Twin:** Generates a highly accurate, CSS-based visual representation of a standard medical folder spine, complete with positional color banding.
* **Time Attack Constraints:** Simulates the fast-paced nature of actual hospital archives with a strict 30-second time limit per folder analysis.
* **Performance Analytics:** Calculates the user's ultimate accuracy (score) and average reaction time, providing actionable metrics for HR and training departments.
* **Interactive Legend Modal:** Features an accessible, on-demand color guide to assist trainees without breaking the simulation flow.
